about

My name is Oliver Haney and I study computer sciences at Stetson University in Deland, Florida. I am a senior, and have learned a lot since I first got into any sort of programming. I mainly specialize in both backend and frontend web application development, but have been recently been branching out into other computer science related fields such as machine learning for business applications, and cybersecurity. Starting this semester, I plan to create an open source security package, containing a password manager, malicious URL detection, and more. I have about three years of experience with Java, and it has quickly become my best language. I am very familiar with the Dropwizard framework, which I use almost daily in the creation of web applications. I have knowledge in other programming languages, such as python, c++, c#, and others, but Java remains my most popular choice.

I became motivated to learn Java, C++, and Python, which I did. I learned more about best programming practices as I attempted to implement more and more complex projects, and discovered new ways to write as little code as possible. I learned that abstraction is a powerful tool, but I still lacked the experience to use it to guide my programming in a meaningful and efficient way. As I accumulated these new skills across a variety of technologies and areas of study, I felt I was ready to apply for more internships. LSQ gave me my first internship opportunity, and it was the most amazing internship. The management including my direct boss were extremely accommodating, encouraging, and generally helpful in my development as a software engineer. I got to seek guidance from some of the most knowledgeable people I have known.
